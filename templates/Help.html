Experiencing trouble buying tickets?

Go through the next steps in order to buy a bus ticket:

Go to B-Ticket / Buy Ticket then select the ticket type and complete the purchase requirements.

If you still experience some kind of problem, please go to the "Report Problem" tab, and fill up the form with the information regarding you specific problem.


Having trouble selecting the payment method?

Go through the following steps in order to select a payment method:

Go to B-Ticket / Buy Ticket fill the presented requirements and press "Complete the purchase".

If you still experience some kind of problem, please go to the "Report Problem" tab, and fill up the form with the information regarding you specific problem.
